Our Sales & Services

Comprehensive automotive diagnostic solutions tailored to your needs

GPS Tracker & Dash Cameras

Professional installation and sales of advanced GPS tracking systems and high-definition dash cameras. Monitor your fleet in real-time, enhance security, and maintain comprehensive video records for safety and insurance purposes.

Vehicle Sensors

Complete sales and service for all types of automotive sensors including oxygen sensors, temperature sensors, pressure sensors, and more. We ensure accurate sensor calibration and replacement for optimal vehicle performance and emissions control.

ECM Boards Sales & Service

Specialized in Engine Control Module repair, reprogramming, and replacement. We offer genuine and aftermarket ECM boards with expert installation and configuration to restore your vehicle's electronic control systems to peak efficiency.

Vehicle Wiring Kit Sales & Service

Comprehensive vehicle wiring solutions including custom harness fabrication, electrical troubleshooting, and complete wiring kit installation. We handle everything from basic repairs to full electrical system overhauls with precision and reliability.

Vehicle Scanning & Diagnostic

Advanced computerized vehicle diagnostics using latest OBD-II and manufacturer-specific scan tools. We identify and troubleshoot engine, transmission, ABS, airbag, and all other electronic system issues with accuracy and speed.

DPF & SCR Complete Solution

Expert Diesel Particulate Filter cleaning, regeneration, and SCR system maintenance. We provide complete solutions for emission control systems including diagnostics, cleaning, repair, and replacement to ensure compliance and optimal performance.

IMMO Programming & Unlocking

Professional immobilizer programming and unlocking services for all vehicle makes. Lost key programming, immobilizer bypass solutions, and security system troubleshooting performed with specialized equipment ensuring vehicle security integrity.

Key Programming

Complete key programming services including transponder key cutting, remote programming, and smart key configuration. We program new keys, duplicate existing ones, and resolve key-related security issues for all vehicle brands.

Vehicle Programming File Solution

Custom ECU programming files, performance tuning, and software updates for enhanced vehicle performance. We provide optimized calibration files for fuel efficiency, power optimization, and emissions control tailored to your specific requirements.
